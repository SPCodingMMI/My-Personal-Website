Who is Xennoh?

I am a Youtuber that does these things called Let's Plays or Walkthroughs where I simply play games of my choice, provide guidance, and commentate my personal experiences. My goal is to hope that a viewer watch the videos and then tries the game out themselves if they have not played it.

Xennoh's Favorite Game Of All-Time

My favorite game of all-time is Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door. As a kid, I played it over non-stop and give myself challenges to make the game more enjoyable. It is also the first game that got me into loving Turn-Based RPGs.

Xennoh's Schedule

I don't really have a schedule to follow with. It was more about when is it necessary to do something. Normally, I do a lot of recording session in one day and then edit my videos. After all of that, I post one video per day.

Xennoh's Free Time

Other than working on my videos, I sometimes do coding to develop skills and knowledge, play games, or chill with my friends on discord.
